Excellent speed/features/print quality/price
PostedAugust 1, 2015
Name: PeteC2

Features 
5 / 5

This replaces a 5+ year old HP 8500 Office Jet. Big footprint for my home office (as with the 8500), but I want a solid, dependable unit, and the 8610 certainly is that.
I was going to spring for the 8620, but at the current price, the 8610 is a stellar bargain.
Set up was reasonably easy BUT - I would recommend that HP go back to the more detailed written installation info that the 8500 series came with. For me, no problem, but I can see where new users might find the set up info lacking.
As I expected, very good print quality both black and color, very good print and copy speed. Fax set up easily and works just fine. ADF works well. Ink economy is also very good, and am pleased at the capacity of the included starter cartridges.
Photo printing is considered a weakness due to the pigment based color inks, but, unless one is looking for absolute top-end photos, this actually does a very credible job with accurate colors...beats any color laser printer for photos by a mile.
I still wish that HP would come out with an optional manual page feed or secondary tray, but aside of that, this is a well designed all-in-one that gets the job done.
